# JOB-SHALA

Job Shala is a Hiring portal where students can look for jobs and internships and recruiters can post openings for the same. It
is a full stack web development project with the following features :-

### 1. Register/Login
Implemented Register and Login functionality for user with help of passport

<img width="1439" alt="Screenshot 2022-09-23 at 8 43 06 PM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/82565121/192049800-84d2a1e2-06b1-4263-84f7-54eb80f133d5.png">



### 2. Apply for Jobs & Internships
Student can apply for jobs and internships easily by just clicking the apply button. Users also have the choice of selecting already uploaded resumes or uploading a new resume.



<img width="1437" alt="Screenshot 2022-09-24 at 1 56 15 AM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/82565121/192051286-9ea97d2b-379c-45e8-be45-7ad46bcc890f.png">



### 3. Search and Filter
Students can search for specific Jobs and Internships according to their preference. 

<img width="1440" alt="Screenshot 2022-09-23 at 8 42 19 PM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/82565121/192051485-9a8adf63-a0e8-4dc0-962f-1f00cefa5934.png">



### 4. Post new Jobs & Internships
Employers can post for a job or internship opening.

Users can review their profile in the dashboard.
